Uni10

Welcome to Uni10, the Universal Tensor Network Library.

Uni10 is programmed in C++ and geared toward applications to the tensor network algorithms, such TEBD, PEPS and MERA. We hope the availability of Uni10 will make future implementation of tensor network algorithms much easier and less error prone.
